Monumental Gaming - Deathrun

Welcome!

Welcome to Monumental Gaming Deathrun. Deathrun is a game of Runners vs Deaths. The goal of a Runner is to make it to the end of the map. The map, in most cases, is just one huge death-trap. To make it to the end, you'll have to somehow survive all the traps activated by the Death players. The goal of a Death is to kill all of the Runners by utilizing the traps within the map. Press F3 in game to purchase items to equip such as hats and custom player models. You will receive points by playing on the server and killing an enemy.
Please follow the below rules and guidelines, to ensure all players have an enjoyable deathrun experience!

1. Server Rules

  • Do not use cheats, hax, scripts or any other type of game modification. Doing so will result in a permanenet ban from the Monumental Community.
  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not ask for a rank up; be it respected, mod, admin or VIP.
  • Do not ask other players for points.
  • Do not harass or bully other players.
    • Doxing (revealing a players real-life identity) a player will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • Do not intentionally annoy people for your own amusement.
  • Do not spam your microphone (this includes continuous noises, talking in a loud manner, playing music through your microphone).
  • Players must not attempt to impersonate Deathrun staff and/or Players in anyway or for any reason (unless you have permission).
  • Points spent in the Points Shop can not be reimbursed by staff.
  • Threatening (or undertaking) to DDOS the server or any of our players will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • When casting a vote kick, discuss why in chat first and give time for the person being kicked to respond e.g. Votekick on Dyoh for button stealing, any objections?
    • Do not start a vote if reasonable objections are raised.
    • Do not start a vote if staff are online.
  • When using any donator commands (!gag, !mute) you must inform staff via admin chat (@) before doing so.

2. Gameplay Rules

  • As a Death, do not give players a free-run.
  • As a Death, you must use traps appropriately and not steal, block or spam them.
  • As a Runner, do not intentionally delay your run.
  • As a Runner, you may skip one trap only.
    • Skipping more than one trap will result in a slay.
  • Bunny-hopping is allowed and can be performed by holding down space, and pressing the a and d keys (provided you have the auto-jump feature enabled in the F1 menu).
  • Do not ghost e.g. you cannot die and then spectate a death and tell runners what platforms are okay to jump on.
  • Do not abuse any glitches or unintentional mechanics.
  • If no staff are online and someone is breaking the rules, take a screenshot/video of the offense and post it on our forums along with their Steam ID and name.
  • Players AFK for three rounds or more will be kicked.
